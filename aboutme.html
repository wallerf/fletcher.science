About Me

Fletcher is a compassionate, community-service minded individual with a deep passion and curiosity for the natural world and all its inhabitants, including people. Since graduating from UVic with a Masters degree in Astrophysics, they have been spending their time volunteering to support community organizers and improve the world around them. This has included sorting, packing and delivering free groceries to folks, providing operational support for markets, arts, and sporting events, and sharing the joys of crafting with others. Fletcher believes in building a better world by getting to know each other, by growing together, and by dreaming together.

As of March 2025, Fletcher is seeking work in administrative roles, where he can use his personability, technology skills, and organization skills to smooth and streamline the operations of local small business, non-profits, or government organizations.

In his personal time, Fletcher is an avid fibre artist, budding gardener, and loves to swim in lakes, oceans, and rivers. He always has some frozen tart shells and fruit sauce ready for a quick dessert to share over tea. They prefer to learn board game rules from people, and have been known to cut roses from the prolific rosebush out front to share with passersby, after removing the thorns, of course.
